Human Computer Interaction

A team of 5 members including myself worked on a project of sketching a mobile app, from low & medium fidelity to higher fidelity. To prototype, software such as Balsamiq, Sketch and Invision were used. The project included design aspects from evaluating usability, user experience and user testing. The app was aimed to increase engagement of students in campus through centralizing all events of the University of Victoria at one place.
